## What It Does (The Features Worth Knowing About)

**Lead capture, everywhere.** Landing pages, forms, surveys, calendars, inbound phone lines — leads flow into one system regardless of where they came from.

**Automated follow-up that doesn't stop.** Once a lead comes in, you can trigger a sequence that goes: forced call → voicemail drop → SMS → email → Facebook Messenger. Set it up once, let it run while you're doing literally anything else.

**Appointment automation.** Calendar with triggers baked in. Someone books? Automated confirmation. Someone doesn't show? Automated no-show sequence fires. You stop babysitting your calendar.

**Unified inbox.** SMS, email, Facebook Messenger all in one place. This sounds minor until you realize how much time you waste switching tabs trying to find which channel a conversation happened on.

**Power dialer.** Work through lead lists faster. Log calls. Trigger automations based on outcomes.

**Reputation management.** Automated review requests to clients after a good interaction. Slow-burn strategy, but it compounds.

**Mobile app.** Full access from your phone. For field agents, this is the difference between a tool you use and one that sits on your desktop collecting dust.

**AI assistant.** Handles initial conversations and keeps leads engaged between your touchpoints — basically your digital front desk.

**52-week nurturing workflow.** Pre-built, already done for you. This alone saves a substantial chunk of setup time.
